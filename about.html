About Me.

Hi there, I'm Noelle!

Welcome to my page! I am a Mathematics with Economics Graduate turned web developer and data science enthusiast. I am best described as someone who likes to learn new skills and have been using my spare time to build a stronger foundation in frontend and backend technologies. I like to code, so I have created a few web applications, which can be viewed on my Github page. My current stack includes HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, SQL, Python and React (but the list is still expanding!)

I am very passionate about equal opportunities. Acknowledging the importance of social responsibility, I launched The Homeless PR (HPR) in 2017 – a project dedicated to raising awareness and tackling the stereotypes associated with Homelessness. To date over 300 care packages have been distributed to the Homeless across the UK and we have exceeded our fundraising targets for our annual Christmas outreach three years in a row! Additionally, I have volunteered for numerous charitable organisations, including the Aleto Foundation, where I oversaw editorial content for their website. I strive to maintain a positive impact, and this is something I will continue to do.

Some of my most notable achievements include the Google Analytics Individual Qualification (2019), being recognised as one of the UK’s Top 100 future leaders of African and Caribbean descent by Powerful Media. This was followed by an invitation to 10 Downing Street by Theresa May (2018) and the "Do well, Do Good" award by Sir Kenneth Olisa OBE, for my contribution to the Aleto Foundation and my work with the Homeless PR.
